Welcome to the Aadhaar Portal

Aadhaar is a 12-digit unique identity number issued to Indian residents by the Government of India. It is based on demographic and biometric data collected by the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). The Aadhaar number serves as proof of identity and address, anywhere in India.

Aadhaar was introduced in 2009, and since then, it has become one of the most important documents for residents of India. It helps in availing government subsidies, financial services, and serves as a digital identity.

What You Can Do on This Portal

  • Create Aadhaar Profile: Fill in your details to create an Aadhaar profile. Once submitted, the form will be locked.
  • View Aadhaar Card: Retrieve and view your Aadhaar details in a card format, stored securely in our cloud database.
  • Download Aadhaar Card: Download your Aadhaar card as a PDF or JPG file for your records.
  • Update Aadhaar Information: Update specific details like your address or image. The changes will be reflected in your Aadhaar card.
  • Delete Aadhaar Profile: Remove your Aadhaar profile entirely from the system, allowing you to create a new profile if needed.
